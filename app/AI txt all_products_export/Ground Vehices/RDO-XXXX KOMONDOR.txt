The Komondor vehicle family is designed and manufactured to meet various customer demands. Based on our experiences, we believe, that most of the costumers would like their vehicle to be at least partly manufactured, assembled or maintained in their countries, so we are ready for the technology transfer.

Key Features:
- Monocoque “V” shaped welded armour steel body under the entire vehicle in general up to: 3a/3b blast protection, up to lvl3 ballistic protection with ballistic glasses, spall-liner inner cover.
- Uniquely designed permanent: 4x4 or 6x6 driveline with independent suspension system.
