Kategória: C4ISR
Termék: PGSR-3iFT ‘BEAGLE’ Mk. II.

PGSR-3iFT ‘Beagle’ Mk. II. is a tower-mounted radar detecting, tracking and classifying targets, moving on or close to the ground.
Designed for desert and harsh environment operation.
Placed inside a hermetically sealed redone to withstand extreme weather conditions.
Mounted on top of towers, 360° coverage, observing and collecting information from the surrounding area.

Key Features:
- Scan Rate: 7°/sec or 14°/sec or 22°/sec
- Range: +/- 10 m at 5 km range
- Azimuth: ≤ 0.3°
- Target Capacity: 600 plots can be displayed
- Frequency Band: X Band (NATO I/J Band)
- Power Requirements: 28 VDC (22–33 VDC) max. 100 W
