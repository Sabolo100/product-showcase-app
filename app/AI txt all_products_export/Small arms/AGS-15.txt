Kategória: Small arms
Termék: AGS-15

This non-lethal pistol was made for home defence.
It is a 2 shots rubber bullet pistol that use 15 mm rubber bullet and 9 mm PAK signal ammunition.

Key Features:
- Calibre: 15 mm rubber bullet /9 mm PAK alarm cartridge
- Kinetic energy up to: 100 Joules (determined by end user)
- Barrel length: 40 mm
- Effective range: 10 – 15 m
