Kategória: Ground Vehices
Termék: RDO-4336 MPV

The RDO-4336 MPV armoured multipurpose  vehicle is a special member of the Komondor light armour vehicle family with interchangeable superstructure system.

Key Features:
- Integrated hydraulic system with: 130 kW power capacities to drive the superstructure or front mounted equipment (water pump, decontamination system, crane, winch, snow blower, mulcher, plow).
- Load capacity: >10 t.
- Towing capacity: >25t.
- Special superstructures (fire-fighter with: 7000-8000l water tank, decontamination, vehicle recovery, mobile HQ, medical evacuation) can be changed by crew without special equipment.
