KEY FEATURES

In the complex environment, not only tasks are practiced, but also the communication and cooperation between different organizations.

CMS includes several subsystems:
    • constructive and virtual simulators,
    • replicas of real systems, such as an Early Warning Monitoring system,
    • on-board reconnaissance software,
    • life detectors etc.


Using data exchange gateways all components see a common virtual environment and exchange data and events.
