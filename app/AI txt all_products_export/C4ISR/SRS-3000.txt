Kategória: C4ISR
Termék: SRS-3000

The receiver consists of the usual components of digital fast-scanning intercept receiver:
-RF front-end,
-HF/VHF digitizer,
-Digital filtering and
-Digital signal processor.
-The SRS-3000 is a frequency-agile, lightweight HF/VHF/UHF receiver designed for limited space and high-mobility application for ground and air environment.
-Its compact size, broad tuning range and high-level remote control functionality make it ideal for multitude of system application, including commercial frequency management and military EW/SIGINT/COMINT missions...

Key Features:
- Up to: 40 MHz wide instantaneous real-time bandwidth.
- Down to: 1 ms time resolution.
- Down to: 1 kHz frequency resolution.
