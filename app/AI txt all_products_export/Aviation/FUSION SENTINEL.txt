Kategória: Aviation
Termék: FUSION SENTINEL

The Fusion Sentinel is a carbon composite aircraft designed for aerial surveillance. 

Equipped with HD and thermal imaging, gyro-stabilized gimbal, and real-time encrypted data transmission up to 100 km, it ensures precision monitoring day and night. 

Ideal for border patrol, reconnaissance, and environmental missions. The Sentinel offers rapid deployment, low operational cost, and versatile payload integration in demanding operational environments.

Key Features:
- Wingspan: 8,44m / 27.69 ft
- Length: 6,62 m / 21.7 ft
- Height: 2,4 m / 7.87 ft
- Cabin Width: 1,17 m / 3.84 ft
- Max Take-off Weight: 600 kg / 1323 lb
- Take-off Distance: 150-250 m / 492-820 ft
- Climb Rate: 4,5-6 m/s/ 900-1200 ft/min
- Max Operating Altitude: 3650 m / 12000 ft
- Stall Speed with Flaps: 83 km/h / 45 kts CAS (LDG Config.)
- Max Cruise Speed: 214-256 km/h 116-122 kts TAS
- Fuel Consumption: 16-21 l/hr / 4.2-5.4 gal/hr
- Manufacturer Rotax: 912-916 IS, ULS
- Horsepower: 100-160 hp
