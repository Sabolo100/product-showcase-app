Kategória: Ground Vehices
Termék: KATICA

KATICA is a versatile unmanned ground vehicle (UGV) platform designed for modern operations.

Its future ready architecture ensures long-term adaptability as mission needs evolve.

The platform’s design strenghts are;
-High payload carriage capacity - enables creating wide-ranging concepts
-Modularity - provides rapid configuration for specific tasks
-Scalability - multiple sizes/classes for diverse profiles
-Adaptability – upgradeable ,future proof system
-Key advantages of using KATICA;
-Being a force multiplier by creating a bigger impact with a fewer personnel,
-Enhancing soldier safety,
-Mission effectiveness,
-Comprehensive ILS,

Key Features:
- Configuration*: 6x6 wheeled chassis
- Length x Width x Height: 3470x 2010x 1000 mm
- Ground Clearance: >30cm
- Weight: 2800kg
- Maximum Payload Weight: up to1500kg
- Top speed (flatsurface): 35km/h
- (terrain): 20km/h
- Power Unit*: Hybrid (diesel+ electric)
- Range: up to35 km electrical+ 110km diesel
- Endurance: 3-4 hours electrical + 5 hours diesel
- Maximum Gradient: 60%
- Maximum Side Slope: 30%
- Fording Depth: ≤ 60 cm
- Obstacle Climbing: ≤ 30 cm
- Trench Crossing: ≤ 90 cm
