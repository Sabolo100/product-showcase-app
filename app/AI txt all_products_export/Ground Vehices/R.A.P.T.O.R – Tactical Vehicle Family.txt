R.A.P.T.O.R. Tactical Vehicle Family, a next-generation lineup of high-performance, multi-role platforms engineered for the toughest battlefields and operational environments. 

The name R.A.P.T.O.R. stands for Rapid Assault Platform for Tactical Operations & Recon, embodying the essence of speed, precision, adaptability, and lethal capability. 

Just like the predator it is named after, the R.A.P.T.O.R. family strikes with speed, hunts with precision, and dominates any terrain. These vehicles combine mobility, modularity, and cutting-edge technology to deliver decisive superiority in combat, reconnaissance, and rescue missions alike.
