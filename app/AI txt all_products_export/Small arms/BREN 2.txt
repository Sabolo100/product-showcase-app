Kategória: Small arms
Termék: BREN 2

The BREN 2 is an automatic weapon that can be used both as a single-shot and as a burst weapon. It has a high-strength, aircraft-grade aluminium alloy frame. Its main parts are both thermally and mechanically resistant. This assault rifle is available in two versions, with 5,56x45mm, 7,62x39mm calibre. Its effective range is 300-500 metres.
