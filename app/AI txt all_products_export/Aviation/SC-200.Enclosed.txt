Kategória: Aviation
Termék: SC-200.Enclosed

The enclosure can reduce overall drag compared to open-cockpit versions, potentially improving performance. Based on the SC-200 model, the enclosed version has an enhanced safety and comfort features:

Comfort and weather features:
-protection from various weather conditions,
-heating system,
-reduced noise and vibration,
-enhanced safety: the enclosed cabin can contribute to overall structural integrity, offers.
-better protection in the event of crash.

Extending the base model (SC-200):
-Police and military application: road and border patrol/surveillance with Radar, Lidar and complex camera system, basic flight training, parachuting.
-Rescue teams: fast medical aid with additional stretcher, search for a missing person.
-Combat wildfires: precision water and retardant drops (~200 litres water tank), support for ground teams, carrying smokejumpers.

Key Features:
- Prototype ready: Development phase
- Empty weight: 298 kg	350 kg
- Rotor diameter: 8.4 m	9.6 m
- Seats: 2	3
- Maximum take-off weight: 560 kg	670 kg
- Maximum take of distance: 120 m	120 m
- Climb rate: 3.5–7 m/s	3.5–7 m/s
- Cruise speed: 140 km/h	140 km/h
- Maximum speed: 165 km/h	165 km/h
- Range: up to 5 hours/500 km (10 hours/1000 km with auxiliary tank)
- Fuel: 95e petrol 60 litres (100 litres with auxiliary tank)
- Dimensions: 8.4 m × 2 m × 2.75 m	9.6 m × 2.5 m × 2.8 m
