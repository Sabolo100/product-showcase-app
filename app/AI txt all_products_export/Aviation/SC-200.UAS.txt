Kategória: Aviation
Termék: SC-200.UAS

The base of the development project is the SC-200 model. The new SC-200.UAS type will be a versatile tool in the field which eliminates human risk and extends application area furthermore in critical operations.
This model will be able to do the following:
-do monitoring (e.g.: power lines, bridges, disaster sites),
-LIDAR-mapping,
-do real-time surveillance and patrolling,
-carrying drones with optical wires
-delivering special packages (fist-aid kit, food, equipment)
-agricultural spraying and monitoring
-do aerial cinematography in filmmaking
-do search and rescue with thermal and high-res cameras.

Key Features:
- Empty weight: 298 kg
- Rotor diameter: 8.4 m
- Seats: 2
- Maximum take-off weight: 560 kg
- Maximum take of distance: 120 m
- Climb rate: 3.5–7 m/s
- Cruise speed: 140 km/h
- Maximum speed: 165 km/h
- Range: up to 5 hours/500 km (10 hours/1000 km with auxiliary tank)
- Fuel: 95e petrol 60 litres (100 litres with auxiliary tank)
- Dimensions: 8.4 m × 2 m × 2.75 m
