Kategória: Aviation
Termék: HUNTEREX

HUNTEREX is a kamikaze mini unmanned aerial vehicle developed for missions requiring rapid response. 

It is resistant to jamming systems and ensures highly reliable communication in electronic warfare environments. 

With its waterproof body, it provides operational durability under challenging terrain and climate conditions. It is a strategic product designed for rapid and high-impact interventions against covert and critical targets in modern warfare tactics.
