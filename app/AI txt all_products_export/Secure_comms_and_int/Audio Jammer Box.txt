Termék: Audio Jammer Box

Confidential conversations have to be protected against information leakage.
The Audio Jammer Box (AJB) provides an ideal solution to isolate the phones/electronic devices from the meeting environment.

Key Features:
- -SG plays: 6 different simultaneous audio tracks, all generated using random music and speech elements from several different languages
