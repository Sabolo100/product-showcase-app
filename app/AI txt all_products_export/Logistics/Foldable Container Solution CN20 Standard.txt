The company offers sustainably foldable container solutions for temporary infrastructure use in 30+ countries worldwide, targeting the event and defense sectors. They won Canadian DND IDEaS' top prize in 2023 and gained "NATO Approved Supplier" certification in 2024.
The CN20 standard combines all the benefits of a 20' ISO fixed frame container with the advantages of foldability, enabling cost-effective transportation and deployment, reliable infrastructure for forces in austere conditions, and NATO interoperability.
