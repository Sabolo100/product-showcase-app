Termék: Bordercam-225

The Bordercam is a mid-range electrooptical camera system. This unique system combines an advanced high-resolution uncooled continuous zoom thermal camera with a mid-range daylight camera on an accurate pan-tilt unit.
-Independent continuous zoom thermal and colour cameras provide maximum situational awareness, user friendliness, and 24/7 threat assessment.
-Ruggedized for harsh environments. The rugged design, certified to IP66, protects the camera against dusty, and other harsh or industrial environments.
-TCP/IP interface, proprietary protocol.

Key Features:
- Parameters: Daylight camera
- Sensor: Global shutter 2/3" CMOS
- Resolution: 2464 × 2056
- Frame rate: Max. 30 fps
- Zoom: 20× optical + 12× digital
- FoV: 38.6–2.05°
- DRI (2.3 × 2.3 m NATO): Detection:30 000 mRecognition: 20 000 mIdentification: 10 000 m
