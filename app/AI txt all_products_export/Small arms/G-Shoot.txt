Kategória: Small arms
Termék: G-Shoot

This non-lethal rifle family was made for crowd control at law enforcement and for home defence at civil use. The G-Shoot is a 2-shot rubber bullet rifle that uses 17,6 mm rubber bullet and .380 signal ammunition.

Key Features:
- Calibre: 17,6 mm rubber bullet /.380 alarm cartridge
- Kinetical energy up to: 120 Joules (determined by end user)
- Barrel length: 480 mm and 280 mm
- Effective range: 20 – 30 m
